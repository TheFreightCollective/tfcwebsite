About The Freight Collective

The Freight Collective was founded on a simple belief: that customers dealing with complex freight deserve a genuine partner, not a call centre. We built the business to handle the jobs that most brokers turn down — urgent, oversized, multi-stage, remote — and to make the whole process straightforward for the people who need it done.

We operate through an extensive national network of trusted transport operators, giving us the reach and flexibility to find the right solution for almost any freight requirement, anywhere in Australia.

Our approach

No two freight movements are the same. A machinery relocation into a remote site requires a completely different approach to an urgent same-day metro delivery — and both require someone who actually knows what they're doing.

We assess every job individually. That means understanding what you're moving, where it needs to go, what access looks like, what the timeline is, and what could go wrong. From there we identify the right operators, coordinate the moving parts, and manage the job through to delivery — keeping you informed along the way.

You deal with one person. We handle everything else.
