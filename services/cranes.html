Cranes & Crane Trucks

Cranes and crane trucks (Hiab vehicles) provide a complete lifting and transport solution in a single operation.

Instead of coordinating multiple contractors, we supply the right vehicle, operator, and lifting capability to get your job completed safely and efficiently.

A Smarter Way to Move and Lift

Crane trucks combine transport and lifting into one streamlined service — ideal for jobs where freight needs to be loaded, unloaded, or placed in position on-site.

This reduces handling, shortens delivery times, and eliminates the need to organise separate cranes or machinery.

What We Can Handle

• Machinery and equipment transport with on-site lifting
• Steel, structural materials, and construction freight
• Site deliveries requiring precise placement
• Palletised freight with no forklift available
• Tight access and urban delivery environments
• Remote or regional deliveries with limited infrastructure

When You Need a Crane Truck

Crane trucks are the ideal solution when:

• There is no forklift or crane on-site
• Freight needs to be lifted into position (not just delivered)
• Access is restricted or space is limited
• You want to reduce handling and risk of damage
• Speed and efficiency are critical

Key Benefits

• Reduced loading and unloading time
• Lower overall job cost compared to separate services
• Improved safety with fewer touchpoints
• Flexible solutions tailored to site conditions
